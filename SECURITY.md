# Security Policy

JobsAI takes the security of our platform and the confidentiality of candidate, customer,
and company data seriously.

## Reporting a vulnerability
If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please report it privately to
**security@jobsai.work**. Do not disclose it publicly until we've had a chance to address it.

Please include:
- a description of the issue and its potential impact,
- steps to reproduce (proof-of-concept if possible),
- any relevant URLs, accounts, or logs (do **not** include real user data or secrets).

## What to expect
- **Acknowledgement** of your report as soon as possible (target: 2 business days).
- An assessment and, where valid, a remediation plan and timeline.
- We'll keep you updated and credit you if you wish, once resolved.

## Scope
Our production services, primarily `app.jobsai.work` and `www.jobsai.work`. Please avoid
testing that could degrade service, access other users' data, or violate privacy. Automated
scanning that generates significant load is not authorized.

## Our commitments
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
- We follow least-privilege access and review it regularly.
- We maintain an incident-response process and will notify affected customers of incidents
  impacting their data without undue delay.

_See our [Privacy Policy](https://app.jobsai.work/privacy) for how we handle personal data._
