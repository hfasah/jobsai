## Residual risk the contract does NOT eliminate (for counsel)
1. **Third parties aren't bound.** Data subjects whose records are searched never signed the agreement; they can still sue, and may name JobsAI. Indemnity only works if the Customer is solvent and the clause holds.
2. **Statutory liability persists.** FCRA (federal) and the RI Identity Theft Protection Act can impose obligations that a private contract cannot waive.
3. **"Neutral tool" weakens if purpose-built for the risky use.** A generic search box reads as a neutral conduit; a "screen this candidate" button reads as facilitation (cf. the Roommates.com line under Section 230). Keep the UI scoped to legitimate own-data/security use.
